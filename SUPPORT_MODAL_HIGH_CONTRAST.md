# 🌟 Support Modal - High Contrast Design

I've updated the modal with much higher contrast colors for better visual impact and readability!

## 🎨 **High Contrast Changes**

### **1. Darker Background**
- ❌ **Before:** `bg-slate-100/50` (light, low contrast)
- ✅ **After:** `bg-slate-900/80` (dark, high contrast)
- **Result:** Modal really pops against the dark background

### **2. Vibrant Gradient Header**
- ❌ **Before:** `bg-slate-50` (plain, low contrast)
- ✅ **After:** `bg-gradient-to-r from-emerald-600 to-emerald-700` (vibrant gradient)
- **Result:** Eye-catching header with strong visual hierarchy

### **3. White Text on Dark Header**
- ❌ **Before:** Dark text on light background
- ✅ **After:** `text-white` on emerald gradient
- **Result:** Excellent readability and modern look

### **4. Contrasting Icon Design**
- ❌ **Before:** White icon on emerald background
- ✅ **After:** Emerald icon on white background
- **Result:** Better visual balance and contrast

### **5. Structured Content Areas**
- ❌ **Before:** Semi-transparent backgrounds
- ✅ **After:** Clear `bg-slate-50` and `bg-white` sections
- **Result:** Better content separation and readability

### **6. Stronger Borders**
- ❌ **Before:** `border-gray-100/50` (very light)
- ✅ **After:** `border-slate-200` (more visible)
- **Result:** Clear visual boundaries

### **7. Deeper Button Color**
- ❌ **Before:** `bg-emerald-500` (lighter)
- ✅ **After:** `bg-emerald-600 hover:bg-emerald-700` (deeper, more contrast)
- **Result:** More prominent call-to-action

## 🎯 **Visual Hierarchy Improvements**

### **Header Section:**
- **Background:** Emerald gradient (`from-emerald-600 to-emerald-700`)

### **Content Section:**
